    About the Campaign

    Green Earth is a global tree plantation initiative dedicated to fighting climate change. Since our start, we've planted over 500,000 trees across 120+ communities and 30+ countries. We believe in restoring habitats for wildlife, and combating global warming.

    • Restoration of natural habitats
    • Improvement of air quality
    • Support for local communities
